*Quick thresholds for classifying training load, recovery, and quality*  

---

## 🔑 Load Management
| Metric     | Green ✅   | Amber ⚠️      | Red ❌         |
|------------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| Strain     | <1,800    | 1,800–2,200   | >2,200         |
| ACWR       | 0.8–1.3   | 1.3–1.5       | >1.5           |
| Monotony   | <1.5      | 1.5–2.0       | >2.0           |

---

## 🔄 Recovery
| Metric         | Green ✅              | Amber ⚠️             | Red ❌                         |
|----------------|-----------------------|----------------------|--------------------------------|
| HRV            | Stable / >50 ms       | ↓15–25% vs baseline  | ↓>25% or <35 ms                |
| Resting HR     | Stable                | +5 bpm               | +10 bpm                        |
| Sleep (avg)    | >8h                   | 7–8h                 | <7h                            |

---

## ⚡ Training Quality  

| Metric                         | Green ✅                      | Amber ⚠️                 | Red ❌                  |
|--------------------------------|-------------------------------|--------------------------|-------------------------|
| Polarisation Index             | ≥0.8                         | 0.6–0.8                  | <0.6                    |
| Durability Index (avgDecoupling) | ≤5%                          | 5–7%                     | >7%                     |
| Quality Session Balance        | ≥2/week (1 long, 1 interval) | 1/week (missing type)    | 0/week (none logged)    |

---

## 🎯 Periodisation
| Phase          | Indicators |
|----------------|------------|
| Build          | CTL ↑ steadily (+3–7/week), ATL <110 |
| Overload       | ACWR >1.3, Strain 1,900–2,200, HRV dip |
| Deload         | Load ↓ ~40%, ATL <80, HRV rebound ≥55 ms |
| Consolidation  | CTL stable ±2, HRV steady, VO₂max preserved |

---

## 🏁 Taper
- Reduce ATL by **40–60%** (10–14 days pre-race)  
- Maintain **intensity** (keep high-intensity, reduce volume)  
- HRV should trend **upward**
